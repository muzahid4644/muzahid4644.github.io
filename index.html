Who I am

Engineer, Maker & Tinkerer

I'm a 3rd-year EEE student at United International University (UIU), Bangladesh. I'm part of the UIU MARINER team — UIU's Advanced Underwater Robotics & Automation Crew — which competed at the MATE ROV World Championship.

Outside of class I run a 3D printing business, build ESP32 gadgets, design custom mechanical keyboards, and play way too much Valorant.
